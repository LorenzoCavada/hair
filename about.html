F.A.Q.

What is HaiR?

HaiR is a service that was born from the work of a team of experts who put the customer's well-being at the center of their work. HaiR allows, through a simple photograph, to understand what is your scalp problem and is able to provide a solution in the shortest possible time.
It's an efficient solution for those who need a quick, reliable and accessible consultation. The technology is based on artificial intelligence which, after processing thousand and thousand of images from a dataset, always growing and updating, identifies the pathology or slight problem that causes your skin disorders and discomfort.

How does HaiR works?

Thanks to an Artificial Intelligence algorithm that use a huge number of past experience, photoes and everything usuful for evaluating each case and identify not only the problem but also the best treatment. All this complicated analysis happen in just a few seconds giving you an almost transparent user experience. Other than that filters will be proposed so that our customers can customize the search and find the most suitable product.

Why should I choose HaiR?

Right now there is not a service like HaiR. The only real alternative are represented by expert with long queue and expansive parcel. HaiR instead is an efficient solution able to give you a quick, reliable and accessible consultation. HaiR team is also very customer oriented and ready to meet any customer needs and doubt, feel free to contact us anytime needed!
